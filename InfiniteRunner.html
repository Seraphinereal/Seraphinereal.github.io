Infinite Runner (untitled)

-=- -=Game Synopsis=- -=-

You play as the 'Freaker' (a stick figure) with at first three health. Projectiles spawn infinitely from the right side of the screen, each having a small chance to be an extra health. The game continuously scrolls to the right through eight different hand-made levels. If you fall behind off screen, miss a jump and fall, or lose all your health, the game restarts itself.

-=- -=Experience=- -=-

"Infinite Runner" was my first solo Unity project, I made it right after my 2 month project I did and made it start to finish in about the span of one week. I am very proud of it for what it is, it's a simple but presentable fun game. I felt like I finished off what I was missing learning wise from the 2 month project. The project didnt require me to make it to the level that I did, I went above and beyond in some degrees to make it look good on a portfolio, and I think for where I am I was correct in doing so.
